❓ Help Center

Find answers to common questions about using TorrentHub.

❓ What is TorrentHub? ▼
TorrentHub is a torrent index and community forum. We do not host any copyrighted files. We only index torrent metadata (file names, sizes, magnet links) that are publicly available. Think of us as a search engine for torrents.
📤 How do I upload a torrent? ▼
Go to the Upload page. You can either upload a file directly (under 10MB) or paste a magnet link. Fill in the title, category, and description, then click Upload. You must be logged in to upload.
⬇️ How do I download a torrent? ▼
On any torrent page, click the big green ⬇️ DOWNLOAD button. This will either redirect you to a magnet link or download a .torrent file. You'll need a torrent client (like qBittorrent, uTorrent, or Transmission) to actually download the content.
👤 How do I create an account? ▼
Click Log In in the top-right corner, then click Register. Fill in your username, nickname, email, and password. You can also sign up with Google or GitHub OAuth.
📚 What is the Forum? ▼
The Forum is a place to discuss topics related to torrents, software, movies, games, and more. You can create topics, post messages, and reply to others. It's like a mini‑Reddit within TorrentHub.
🏆 What is the Top 100 page? ▼
The Top 100 page ranks users by reputation. You earn reputation when others like your torrents, forum posts, or comments. The higher your reputation, the higher you rank.
🔔 How do notifications work? ▼
You'll receive notifications when someone votes on your content, comments on your posts, sends you a message, or follows you. Check the 🔔 bell icon in the header for real‑time alerts, or visit the Notifications page.
💬 How do I send a private message? ▼
Go to someone's profile and click the 💬 Message button. You can also go to the Messages page and start a new conversation with any user ID. You can even attach files (images, PDFs, etc.) to your messages.
🛡️ What is the Admin Panel? ▼
The Admin Panel is only visible to administrators and moderators. It allows you to manage users (ban/unban), approve reported torrents, and view site statistics. If you're not an admin, you won't see this option.
🎨 How do I customize my profile? ▼
Go to your Account → Edit Profile. You can upload an avatar, a cover image, add a bio, location, and social links. You can also toggle Do Not Disturb and Allow Messages from there.
🌙 How do I enable Dark Mode? ▼
Go to Settings and toggle the Enable Dark Mode switch. Your preference is saved and applied across all pages.
📄 Are there Terms of Service? ▼
Yes! You can read our Terms of Service and Piracy / DMCA Policy at any time. We take copyright seriously and comply with the DMCA.
