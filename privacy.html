🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

At TorrentHub, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and hashed password.
  • Profile Data: Optional information you provide (avatar, bio, location, social links).
  • Content: Torrent uploads, forum posts, comments, and messages you create.
  • Activity Data: Votes, follows, and interactions with other users' content.
  • Session Data: Session tokens and login timestamps to keep you logged in.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and improve the Service (torrent indexing, forum, messaging).
  • Authenticate your account and maintain session security.
  • Personalize your experience (dark mode preferences, themes).
  • Display reputation scores and badges on your profile.
  • Send notifications about interactions (comments, votes, messages).

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a Cloudflare D1 database. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your public profile information (username, nickname, reputation, badges) is visible to other users as part of the community experience.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage in your browser to save settings (dark mode preference, theme color, background image) and your session token. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your data by logging into your account.
  • Edit: Update your profile information anytime.
  • Delete: Delete your account (button in Account).
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (contact us via Messages).

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 7 days.

8. Third-Party Services

We use OAuth providers (Google, GitHub) for optional login. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not store your OAuth tokens beyond what's necessary for authentication.

9. Children's Privacy

TorrentHub is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users of any significant changes via a notice on the website.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through the Forum or Messages system.
