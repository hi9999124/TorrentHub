🚫 Piracy Policy & DMCA

Last Updated: July 12, 2026
🔴 Important: TorrentHub does NOT host any copyrighted files on our servers. We are a torrent indexer — we only index publicly available torrent metadata. We take copyright infringement seriously and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

1. No Hosting of Copyrighted Content

TorrentHub is a torrent metadata indexer and community forum. We do NOT store, host, or distribute any copyrighted files, movies, music, software, or other protected content. All torrent files and magnet links are provided by third-party users and are not stored on our servers.

We are similar to a search engine — we index links to third-party content that is publicly available on the internet.

2. DMCA Compliance

We comply with the provisions of the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe that your copyrighted work has been infringed upon in any way through our Service, please submit a DMCA takedown notice to us.

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, we will take down the specific torrent metadata in question and notify the uploader.

3. How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

To file a DMCA notice, please provide the following information in writing:

  • Your contact information: Full name, address, phone number, and email address.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work: A description of the work you claim has been infringed.
  • Identification of the infringing material: The URL(s) of the torrent metadata on TorrentHub that you want removed.
  • A statement of good faith: A declaration that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  • Accuracy statement: A statement that the information in your notice is accurate, under penalty of perjury.
  • Your signature: Electronic signature (typing your full name is acceptable).

Send your notice to: dmca@torrenthub.com (this is a placeholder email — in production, use a real contact).

4. Counter-Notification

If you believe that your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification. We will comply with the DMCA procedures and may restore the content if a valid counter-notice is received, unless the copyright owner files a court action.

5. Repeat Infringers

We maintain a policy of terminating accounts of users who are repeat infringers. If a user receives multiple valid DMCA notices, we will terminate their account and remove all their content.

6. No Liability

TorrentHub is not liable for any third-party content indexed on our platform. We act as an intermediary and do not have control over the content uploaded by users. All responsibility lies with the uploader.

7. Good Faith & Reporting

We encourage the community to report any infringing content. Users can report torrents by contacting our moderation team via the Forum.
